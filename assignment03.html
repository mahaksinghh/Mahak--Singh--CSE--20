ABES Engineering College – History

ABES Engineering College, Ghaziabad was established in the year 2000 with the objective of providing quality technical education. The college is affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow and approved by AICTE.
